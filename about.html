About JR Industrial

A Stoke-on-Trent industrial supplies company with over 25 years of experience serving businesses across the UK.

Who We Are

JR Industrial Ltd is a specialist supplier of PPE, workwear, safety equipment, janitorial supplies and workplace essentials. Based at Metro Business Park in Stoke-on-Trent, we supply businesses of all sizes across the UK — from sole traders to national organisations.

We stock products from trusted brands including Portwest, 3M, DuPont/Tyvek, CAT Footwear, Alpha Solway, ORN, Silverline and more. Our customers order through our dedicated portal at jrindustrialportal.co.uk, with APC Overnight delivery across the UK.

Whether you need a single pair of safety boots or a full fleet of branded workwear, we're here to help. Call us on 01782 262888, Monday to Friday, 8am–5pm.
