Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

JR Industrial Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is a company registered in England and Wales. Our registered address is Unit J1 Metro Business Park, Clough Street, Stoke-on-Trent, ST1 4AF. You can contact us at sales@jrindustrial.co.uk or by calling 01782 262888.

2. What Data We Collect

When you use the enquiry form on this website, we collect your name, email address, phone number and any information you include in your message. We do not collect payment card data through this website — all orders are processed via the customer portal.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information you submit to respond to your enquiry and, where you have indicated interest, to provide information about our products and services. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Legal Basis

We process your personal data on the basis of legitimate interests (responding to your enquiry) and, where applicable, consent.

5. Data Retention

We retain enquiry data for up to 24 months, after which it is securely deleted unless a contractual relationship requires longer retention.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at sales@jrindustrial.co.uk.

7. Third-Party Services

This website is hosted on Netlify, which processes form submissions on our behalf. Netlify's privacy policy is available at netlify.com/privacy. We use Google Fonts, which may log your IP address when loading font files.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at jrindustrial.co.uk/privacy.
